Project Proposal & Presentation

A project proposal is critical for allocating time and resources to ensure that the project is completed to a high standard. The proposal phase is the initial assessment for the CDAP module. Students must submit four individual proposal reports Â describing each component and the proposed system in detail. The proposal presentation is examined by an evaluation panel comprised of two examiners and a moderator.

Date: 8th March 2021
Allocated Marks: 10%

Progress Presentation 1

Presentation of progress 1 assessment is critical for ensuring that students are on course to solve the problem presented. Students must demonstrate that they have completed 50% of the tasks in the proposed system. The progress presentation is examined by an evaluation panel comprised of two examiners and a moderator.

Date: 5th July 2021
Allocated Marks: 10%

Progress Presentation 2

Students must complete the project and demonstrate 90 percent completion at the progress presentation 2. The progress presentation is examined by an evaluation panel comprised of two examiners and a moderator.

Date: 14th October 2021
Allocated Marks: 18%

Camera Ready Poster Demo

Posters are frequently utilized in academia, and the majority of conferences include poster sessions as part of their program. Findings posters clearly and beautifully explain information or research in order to help advertise it and spark discussion.

Date: 14th October 2021

Final Presentation & Viva

At the final presentation, students are expected to show and display the completed product. And viva is a one-on-one oral session between members of the project group and the assessment panel during which members of the project group respond to particular questions posed by the panel. The final presentation and viva are examined by an evaluation panel comprised of two examiners and a moderator.

Date: 22nd November 2021
Allocated Marks: 25%
